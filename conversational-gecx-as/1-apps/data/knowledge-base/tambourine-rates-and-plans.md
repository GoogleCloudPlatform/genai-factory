# Rates and Service Tiers

This internal guide details the standard tariff structures and subscription tiers for **Tambourine Mobile**. Support specialists should use this data to assist customers in selecting the most efficient tier for their usage patterns. Tambourine Mobile operates on a high-capacity infrastructure designed for low latency and high-volume data throughput.

## Pay-Per-Use Standard Rates

For subscribers not enrolled in a recurring tier or those who have exceeded their tier's hard limits, the following rates apply. All billing is processed in USD.

* **Standard Voice (Domestic)**: $0.18 per minute
* Billed in 30-second increments, rounded up.
* Excludes high-priority concierge lines and overseas routing.


* **Standard Text (Domestic)**: $0.08 per outgoing message
* Incoming messages are processed at no cost.
* Encrypted messaging via data does not count toward this total.


* **On-Demand Data**: $0.07 per MB
* Calculated per KB.
* Automatic "Safety Buffer" pauses data once $50.00 of overages are reached unless manually overridden.

## Subscription Tiers

Tambourine Mobile offers four distinct tiers tailored for different user profiles. All tiers include access to the Tambourine 5G+ network where available.

### 1. The Entry Point

* **Recurring Cost**: $30.00/month
* **Initial Setup**: $5.00
* **Core Features**:
* **Data**: 8 GB Ultra-Speed Data
* **Text**: Unlimited Domestic
* **Voice**: 300 Minutes



**Terms**: Unused minutes do not carry over. Once the 8 GB threshold is reached, data is throttled to 64 kbps. This tier is restricted to smartphone use only; tethering is disabled.

### 2. The Streamer’s Choice

* **Recurring Cost**: $50.00/month
* **Initial Setup**: $0
* **Core Features**:
* **Data**: 35 GB Ultra-Speed Data
* **Text**: Unlimited Domestic
* **Voice**: Unlimited Domestic
* **Perk**: Optimized HD Video Streaming (up to 1080p)



**Terms**: After 35 GB of consumption, speeds are adjusted to 512 kbps. Subject to the Tambourine Network Integrity Policy, which monitors for automated bot behavior or non-human traffic patterns.

### 3. The Global Executive

* **Recurring Cost**: $90.00/month
* **Initial Setup**: $0
* **Core Features**:
* **Data**: 150 GB Ultra-Speed Data
* **Text**: Unlimited Global (200+ countries)
* **Voice**: Unlimited Domestic + 500 Overseas Minutes
* **Perk**: 5 GB Monthly International Data Roaming



**Terms**: The 150 GB pool is eligible for "Velocity Rollover," allowing up to 20 GB of unused data to move to the next month. International minutes apply to landlines and mobiles in designated Zone A countries.

### 4. The Multi-Link (Family/Business)

* **Recurring Cost**: $120.00/month (Includes 2 Primary Lines)
* **Initial Setup**: $40.00
* **Core Features**:
* **Data**: 250 GB Shared Ultra-Speed Pool
* **Text**: Unlimited Domestic (All Lines)
* **Voice**: Unlimited Domestic (All Lines)
* **Add-ons**: Add up to 6 additional lines for $25.00/line.



**Terms**: All lines share the 250 GB pool. If the pool is exhausted, all connected devices move to 1 Mbps speeds. "Primary Line" holders can set data caps for individual "Secondary Lines" via the Tambourine Control App.

## Specialized Service Plans

### The Developer’s Data-Only

* **Recurring Cost**: $45.00/month
* **Activation**: $0
* **Inclusions**: 60 GB Ultra-Speed Data. No voice or text capabilities.
* **Device Focus**: Tablets, specialized IoT hardware, and mobile hotspots.

### The Scholar Sync

* **Recurring Cost**: $28.00/month
* **Activation**: $0 (With verified .edu email)
* **Inclusions**: 12 GB Ultra-Speed Data, Unlimited Text, 1000 Minutes.
* **Bonus**: Zero-rated data for specific educational platforms and research databases.
